About Me

Hi! My name is Matt, and I'm 31 years old. I was born on the Fourth of July in 1986; the 100th anniversary of the Statue of Liberty. I was born in Manhattan, but my family moved to New Hampshire less than a year after I was born. I've lived in New Hampshire for most of the rest of my life, starting in Cheshire county and then the seacoast area before finally settling in Concord.


I recieved my Bachelor of Science at UNH, and my MBA at Antioch NE Grad School. I worked many odd jobs throughout my life, but recently I started a custom drum building company called BoomDog Drums, worked as a recycling technician, and volunteered at an elementary school in Israel. Currently, I am a Lyft driver part time so I can spend time learning coding and hopefully start a new career. Thanks for looking!

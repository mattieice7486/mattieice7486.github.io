Who am I?

I studied web development at the UNH Coding Bootcamp in 2018. I learned the MERN stack, which stands for Mongo.db, Express, React, and Node.js.

I earned my Green MBA at Antioch NE Graduate School in 2011. This was a master's program to merge the concepts of sustainable growth and business.

From 2013 - 2017 I worked as a recycling technician as well as a lab tester at a telecommunications company.

I love to travel. I studied abroad in Israel and New Zealand, and have been all over North America and Europe.

Music runs deep. I started a drum building company called BoomDog Drums and played bass in a band called Dive Team.
